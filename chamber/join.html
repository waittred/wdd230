Join the Seattle Chamber of Commerce

Nonprofit Membership:

Connect, Collaborate, Contribute!

Join our vibrant community of nonprofit organizations dedicated to making a positive impact in Seattle. For just $500 annually, Nonprofit Members enjoy exclusive networking opportunities, access to valuable resources, and the chance to collaborate with like-minded organizations. Amplify your mission and elevate your reach by becoming an integral part of the Seattle Chamber of Commerce.

Bronze Membership:

Essential Foundations for Business Growth!

The Bronze Membership, priced at $1250 annually, is your gateway to foundational support for business success. Enjoy strategic networking events, marketing exposure, and essential resources designed to give your business a solid start. As a Bronze Member, you're laying the groundwork for growth and positioning your brand prominently within the Seattle business landscape.

Silver Membership:

Unlock Opportunities, Accelerate Growth!

Silver Membership, available at $2500 annually, is designed for businesses ready to take their success to the next level. Enjoy enhanced visibility, exclusive educational resources, and premium networking opportunities. With Silver, you're positioned to accelerate your growth, connect with key influencers, and stay ahead in the dynamic Seattle business ecosystem.

Gold Membership:

Premier Partnership for Unparalleled Success!

Experience the pinnacle of Chamber membership with Gold. Priced at $5000 annually, Gold Membership offers top-tier networking events, personalized business support, and exclusive access to influential leaders. Elevate your brand to new heights, showcase your commitment to excellence, and position your business as a leader in the Seattle business community. Gold Membership is your key to unparalleled success.
