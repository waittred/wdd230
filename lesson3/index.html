Purpose

The purpose of creating this website serves four purposes:

  • Assist in the development of commerce, trade, and industry in Colorado Springs
  • Provide support or oppose legislation that may affect our members
  • Provide information to members about their products or services they offer
  • Organize trade fairs and other various events within our community
