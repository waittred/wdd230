Description of the Target Audience

The target audience are individuals who are interested in what our Chamber Memebers are selling. These target individuals are seeking out something to purchase to solve an issue that a service or product will solve. Additionally, our website will offer visibility, credibility, and support for new or growing businesses within our community.
